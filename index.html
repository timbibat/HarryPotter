About The Project

This Harry Potter Directory project is a tribute to the beloved wizarding world created by J.K. Rowling. It serves as a comprehensive archive of characters from the Harry Potter series, providing fans and enthusiasts with detailed profiles of students, staff, and magical beings associated with Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The directory is designed to be user-friendly and visually appealing, allowing users to easily explore and learn more about their favorite characters.
